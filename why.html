Summary of top 3 reasons we’re having a cloud wedding.

We want more of you to be able to join us!

In a world of tight budgets and tight schedules, we want to bring down barriers for friends around the world to join us in celebrating love and life. Raise your glass for a globally distributed, simultaneous toast!

We believe wedding expenses can be a source of change the world! How?

Weddings cost money — for hosts to organize, and for guests to go for. What if some of this money was spent on helping people overcome hardship? Caring for animals? Or create change? Every year, it is estimated that 2.5 million weddings happen in the United States alone, spending $53 billion a year. If only 1% of wedding expenses were spent on causes, it would mean $530 Million a year going to causes! This can happen when more couples allocate some of their wedding budgets to causes. This can also happen by having only 1% of newlyweds choose an alternative way to have a wedding...

Enter ' Cloud Wedding '! We want an alternative wedding experience that

  1. Makes it easy for friends from around the world to participate!
  2. Integrates some of the fun of traditional weddings (Photo-taking, chatting and catching up with old friends)
  3. Create a special shared experience for everyone

Wouldn't it be fun to try this out?

This is a social experiment, culture evolving. While it may be different, we hope you are open to a new and unique experience! In the same way, online booking, online commerce, eBooks, online businesses, online dating, online document signing are so common today, who knows if cloud weddings will someday be an additional way for more people to celebrate love and life more often!

What is a cloud wedding?

It is a wedding that is hosted on ‘the cloud‘. In other words, an online wedding! :)

Am I physically attending the wedding?

Not for this one. We want to keep our environmental impact low while giving you, our guests, a front seat to our marriage.

Where will you (bride and groom) both be?

We will be showing up on your computer / iphone screen via a live video! The live video will be available on the website on the day itself so please RSVP!

What equipment do I need?

Any phone, a desktop computer, or a laptop will do. Just make sure your speakers are working as there will be voice and music!

What do I (as a guest) have to do during the wedding?

Kick back, relax, and tune in at the right time - from wherever you are!

Will guests be able to see me during the wedding?

We will not be able to view you, and neither will the other guests. Our chat function will be available for us to read while our dedicated host will be facilitating the cloud wedding.

Do I have to dress up for the wedding?

Because we will not be able to view you, and neither will the other guests, there is no need to dress up. But if you really want to dress up, it can be fun, but it is not required!

What will happen during the wedding?

Just like any wedding, you’ll only find out… when you are there! You can however expect the various elements that make weddings fun to be recreated for you!

Can my plus 1 be there too?

The more the merrier! Please make sure they RSVP.

So each guest must have a separate account?

Yes. They will need a separate account to be present at the cloud wedding.

How long will the cloud wedding be?

Approximately 90 mins.

I would like to give you a wedding gift! How should I pass it to the both of you?

Thank you so much! We would really appreciate it if you could kindly extend your generosity towards the causes we fully believe and have supported. You will be able to do this on the day, during the wedding.

Is there a time a slot?

Yes, our cloud wedding will be at 5.00PM (Vancouver, Canada Time). To see what time it will be where you are, please click here.

Will I be able to watch the video after the cloud wedding event is over?

Unfortunately, you will not be able to. We will be live streaming on the day, so please do be present to experience it!

Has this been done before?

Yes, we learned about this concept from our beloved friends, K & E!

So, this is the first time it’s ever been done?

Been done before especially during the pandemic. We’d like to see this used by everyone

What will happen after the cloud wedding?

We set off to enjoy our time as newlyweds and we will send our thanks personally as soon as we meet you around the globe!
